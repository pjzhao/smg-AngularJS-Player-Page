FAQ


How to rate a game?

You may rate the games in the game stats page (1.0 ~ 5.0).
Your feedback may make other players find out their enjoyable games.
The developers appreciate your feedback to improve the games.

How to start play?

After choosing game, press auto-match button. If some player is waiting for an opponent, the match starts.
You will be added to the waiting list if nobody is available while other players may send request to play with you.

How to earn more tokens?

Play more, win the bets and you may also purchase the tokens via Facebook payment.

What can I do with tokens?

Every time, you play a game. You may win or lose your tokens. You may use your tokens as your bets.
Clearly, your bets may help you attract more players to play with you.
More tokens, more chance!
