Channel Overview

Login

Our web-site is free for players and developers! Developers and players share the same log in page. You can register and log in with
your Google account, FaceBook account, or our web-site account.

Sign Up

After registering and logging in our website, you can easily manage your personal info. and play history. You can invite your friends
to share fun and pleasure with you.

Game history

You can get your game history in different ways: hitory of one particular game; your highest score; your favorite game; and game that
you play best...
If you hope to get more info., just tell us. We will make it.

Choose Game

You can choose game through our recommendation link. You can also choose game through our game list page. You can sort games in
different ways.
Choose you like, and we will help you find out what you really want.

FAQ

How to rank a game?

You may rank our games in the game detail page. The highest score is 5. Your feedback may make other players find out their enjoyable
games easier, and help our developers improve our games. We really appreciate your feedback

How to start a game?

You may go to the game page through the recommendation link, or figure out your game from the game list.

How to start a game?

After choosing game, press auto-match button. If some player is waiting for an opponent, you may play with that player. If there is nobody
waiting there, you will be added to the waiting list. And other players may send request to play with you.

How to get more tokens?

Play more, or join games with higher bets.
But, please remember this: you may lose it, too.

What can I do with tokens?

Every time, you play a game. You may win or lose your tokens. You may use your tokens as your bets. Clearly, your bets may help you
attract more players to play with you.
More tokens, more chance!
